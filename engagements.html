Engagements

How a client works with Vector.

Vector operates as a managed analytics service. The systems remain behind the work, while the client receives structured analysis, scenario support, and regular advisory conversations tailored to the problem at hand.

This arrangement is intentional. Most teams do not need another tool to learn. They need clear answers, readable assumptions, and disciplined follow-through.

01
Typical Engagement Structure

Initial Consultation

Define the race, policy question, organizational goal, timeline, and available inputs.

Model Construction

Build the working framework, assumptions, and opening scenario set.

Ongoing Analysis

Provide regular readouts, updates, and scenario refreshes as conditions evolve.

Advisory Discussion

Translate outputs into choices leadership can actually act on.

02
What a Client Can Expect
Readable Analysis

Clear language, explicit assumptions, and outputs that can be challenged rather than accepted on faith.

Confidential Handling

Client information, datasets, and strategic discussions are treated as confidential engagement material.
