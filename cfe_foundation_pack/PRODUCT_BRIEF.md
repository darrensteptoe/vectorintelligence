# Campaign Finance Engine (CFE) — Product Brief

## Product name
Campaign Finance Engine (CFE)

## One-line definition
A standalone campaign budget, funding path, and finance execution system that helps campaigns model all major costs, phase them over time, compare them to historical norms, and manage the fundraising activity required to safely fund the plan.

## Relationship to Field Path Engine
CFE is the finance sibling to FPE.

- FPE models what the campaign plans to do on the ground and what that plan requires operationally.
- CFE models what the campaign must spend across all major functions, when those costs hit, what must be raised to cover them, and whether the campaign is on track to support the plan.

## Central question
What is this campaign trying to fund, when do those costs hit, how much money must be raised by each checkpoint, what does history say is realistic for this kind of race, and is the campaign on track to cover the plan?

## Primary users
- Finance Director
- Candidate
- Campaign Manager
- Senior Consultant
- Treasurer / compliance-adjacent operator

## What the product must do well
1. Build a real campaign budget.
2. Turn that budget into a time-aware spend curve.
3. Translate spend timing into raise timing.
4. Use historical finance evidence as a realism layer.
5. Track the activity that produces money.
6. Explain who is funding the campaign and where support is coming from.
7. Produce reports that can be circulated immediately.

## What the product is not
- not a generic bookkeeping tool
- not a compliance filing portal
- not a generic donor CRM
- not a generic calendar
- not a clone of NGP

## Product pillars
1. Budget Planning
2. Spend Timing and Reserve Planning
3. Historical Finance Intelligence
4. Funding Path Generation
5. Finance Operations
6. Donor and Expenditure Intelligence
7. Reporting and Risk Control
