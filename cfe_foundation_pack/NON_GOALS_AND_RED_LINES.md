# NON_GOALS_AND_RED_LINES.md

## Non-goals
CFE is not being built as:
- a full compliance filing replacement
- a general accounting ledger
- a generic donor CRM replacement
- a generic scheduling app
- a live mutable bridge into FPE internals
- a speculative AI classifier that hides uncertainty

## Red lines
- No page-level recomputation of canonical engine metrics.
- No hiding unknowns to make dashboards look cleaner.
- No fake precision in donor income or occupational inference.
- No design drift that makes CFE feel like a different product family from FPE.
- No bridge behavior that creates circular dependencies.
- No report filler language.
- No “smart” warnings without clear canonical triggers.
