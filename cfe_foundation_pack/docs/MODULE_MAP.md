# Campaign Finance Engine (CFE) — Module Map

## Module 1 — Race and Campaign Setup
Defines race, election structure, filing environment, and campaign context.

## Module 2 — Campaign Budget Builder
Models the full campaign budget across all major domains.

## Module 3 — Spend Timeline Engine
Converts static budget lines into a month-aware spend curve with peak windows and commitment pressure.

## Module 4 — Historical Disclosure Intelligence
Ingests and preserves public campaign finance evidence.

## Module 5 — Comparable Race Engine
Determines which prior races matter as historical analogs.

## Module 6 — Benchmark Engine
Turns historical evidence into practical thresholds.

## Module 7 — Funding Path Engine
Determines what must be raised, by when, to safely fund the plan.

## Module 8 — Channel Allocation Engine
Allocates the funding requirement across finance channels.

## Module 9 — Finance Operations Layer
Tracks call time, donor meetings, fundraisers, pledges, tasks, and filing pushes.

## Module 10 — Planned vs Actual Engine
Measures execution against plan and explains why the campaign is ahead or behind.

## Module 11 — Donor Intelligence Layer
Explains who funds the campaign and from where.

## Module 12 — Expenditure Intelligence Layer
Explains where money is going and whether it aligns with strategy.

## Module 13 — Risk Console
Warns leadership before problems become obvious.

## Module 14 — Reporting Engine
Generates consultant-grade internal and candidate-facing outputs.

## Module 15 — Scenario Engine
Stress-tests alternate paths without changing the canonical baseline.

## Module 16 — FPE ↔ CFE Bridge Layer
Imports field-demand snapshots and exports field-funding status snapshots.
