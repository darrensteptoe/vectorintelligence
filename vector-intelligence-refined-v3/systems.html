Vector Intelligence operates a line of internal platforms that support our work across campaign strategy and policy environments.

These systems are not the point of the firm; they are the infrastructure behind the work. Their role is to help Vector see patterns, surface pressure points, and evaluate paths with greater rigor than conventional advisory work typically allows.

System Line
System 01
Campaign Intelligence Platform

Used to evaluate campaign position, test strategic assumptions, and clarify what must be true for a race to move in the desired direction.

It supports work across campaign analysis, operational pacing, strategic comparison, and cycle-long decision support.

System 02
Legislative Intelligence Platform

Used to monitor policy environments, interpret legislative movement, and identify the developments most likely to affect strategy.

It supports work in public affairs, stakeholder analysis, issue monitoring, and broader policy evaluation where context matters as much as activity.

What Clients Should Understand
