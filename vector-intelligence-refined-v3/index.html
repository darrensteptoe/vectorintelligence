Vector Intelligence is a managed intelligence and analytics firm built for campaigns, public affairs teams, and organizations operating in high-stakes political environments.

We combine analytical judgment with proprietary systems to help clients see their position more clearly, evaluate strategy more rigorously, and move with greater confidence when conditions change.

From electoral campaigns to legislative environments, Vector is built to answer the same underlying question: what is happening, what matters most, and what path forward is most realistic?

What Vector Does
Campaign Strategy Analysis

Vector helps campaigns understand where they stand, what must change, and which strategic choices are most likely to improve the outcome of a race.

Field & Operational Assessment

We evaluate whether campaign plans are aligned with the scale and pace of the work required, so leadership can act before small problems become structural ones.

Scenario & Decision Support

Vector gives clients a structured way to compare paths, pressure-test assumptions, and understand how different choices may alter the strategic landscape.

Legislative Monitoring

We track evolving policy environments and help clients understand how legislative movement, stakeholder activity, and timing can create risk or opportunity.

Policy Intelligence

Vector provides higher-level analysis of legislation and public policy dynamics, helping organizations see beyond alerts and summaries to the larger strategic picture.

Ongoing Strategic Support

Our work is designed to support decision-making over time, with recurring analysis and clear points of view as campaigns and policy environments evolve.
