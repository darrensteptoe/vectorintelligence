What Vector Does
Small firm.
Serious systems.

Campaigns and policy organizations often operate with incomplete information. Plans are built on assumptions about turnout, persuasion, staffing, timing, and legislative dynamics that are rarely tested before money and effort are committed.

Vector Intelligence provides structured analysis that helps decision-makers answer three practical questions: what outcome are we trying to reach, what conditions are required to reach it, and does the current strategy realistically produce those conditions?

The objective is not more dashboards. It is clearer judgment.

03
Intelligence Systems
Campaign Intelligence System

A modeling framework used to evaluate electoral strategy. The system integrates voter file data, turnout baselines, field capacity, persuasion assumptions, and budget constraints into one decision model.

The output is a plain strategic question: can the current plan actually produce enough votes to win?

Legislative Intelligence System

A policy intelligence framework used to analyze legislation and the environments around it. The system combines current law, proposed bills, amendments, lobbying activity, campaign finance relationships, and demographic context.

Instead of merely tracking a bill, it helps explain what is changing, who is pushing the change, and where the pressure points are.
