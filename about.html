About

An independent firm built around structured judgment.

Vector Intelligence is meant to read as it actually is: a small specialist analytics shop that develops and operates focused intelligence systems for campaigns and policy environments.

The firm is not founder-forward by design. The emphasis stays on the work, the models, and the quality of the readout.

01
Operating View
Clear models improve decisions.
Vector is organized around a simple premise: people make better strategic decisions when the structure of a problem is made visible. That is true in campaigns, and it is true in legislative environments.
The firm’s role is not to replace experience or political judgment. It is to give judgment a better frame: explicit assumptions, visible constraints, and more realistic readings of what a plan can actually accomplish.
